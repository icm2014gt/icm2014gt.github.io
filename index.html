ICM2014 Satellite Conference on Extremal and Structural Graph Theory

Archived

In Year 2014, International Congress of Mathematicians (ICM) will be held in Korea during August 13-21, 2014. Before this big event, we would like to have a satellite conference on extremal and structural graph theory.

You may still register but the hotel booking for this conference is now closed.
