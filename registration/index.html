Registration

If you want to attend ICM2014 Satellite Conference on Extremal and Structural Graph Theory, please register until April 30, 2014. We extend the deadline for registration. Please register until May 15, 2014.

There will be the conference registration fee. We will later email you about the payment, perhaps in May or June. The registration fee does not include the accommodation.

As of May 20, we decided to have no registration fee. Participants will be asked to pay for their own meals. To book an accommodation, please see the Accommodation page.
